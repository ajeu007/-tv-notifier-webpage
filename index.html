TV Notifier App

Welcome to the official page of the TV Notifier app! Stay up to date with the latest TV series, films, and streaming events right from your macOS menu bar.

The TV Notifier app helps you receive notifications whenever your favorite shows or movies have new updates or releases.

Features:

  • Get notified when new episodes are available.
  • Enter your favorite shows, films, or events to track.
  • Simple and easy-to-use menu bar application.
